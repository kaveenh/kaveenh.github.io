Research

My research focuses on Data Readiness for AI (DRAI), aiming to build scalable, trustworthy systems that ensure data is usable and effective for AI-driven applications. Recent work includes:

  • Developing frameworks such as AIDRIN (AI Data Readiness Inspector) to assess and quantify data readiness for AI workflows (SSDBM 2024, SSDBM 2025).
  • Conducting a comprehensive survey and introducing dimensions of data readiness, covering core concepts, challenges, and current evaluation approaches (ACM Computing Surveys 2025).
  • Integrating data readiness assessments into privacy-preserving federated learning (PPFL) systems (IEEE e-Science 2025).
